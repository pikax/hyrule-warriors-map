    Adventure Map

    The Adventure Map is the first map that you have access to in Adventure Mode. For starters, we'll go over which Item Cards are available in the Adventure Map, what they do, and where to get them. If you ever need find yourself needing a particular Item Card, this will be a good quick reference chart for where to look for one. Always remember that Item Cards cannot always be obtained so you might have to wait a few battles before an Item Card is available again.

    To find the scenario that you want information about, look for its location in the grid below and click on the name to jump directly to that scenario's unlockables. In the release, certain color areas only open up as you progress though Legend Mode. The black squares are initially open and other squares are revealed in order of increasing difficulty. The green squares are accessible after completing Prologue - The Sorceress of the Valley. The yellow squares are accessible after completing Ocarina of Time - The Water Temple. The purple squares are accessible after completing Twilight Princess - The Shadow King. The orange squares are accessible after completing Skyward Sword - Sealed Ambition. The blue squares are accessible after completing Epilogue - Shining Beacon. The final red squares are accessible after completing Grand Finale - Liberation of the Triforce. In the release, you can access all areas by simply completing The Armies of Ruin.
